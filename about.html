    I concentrate on recent advances in techniques related to simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM). My researches include:

     1) fast SLAM for VR/AR on mobile devices (with external supports), e.g., ROSAC,

     2) robust SLAM in complex environments, e.g., LOOF,

     3) collaborative SLAM based on multiple sensors and multiple unmanned systems.

    The goal of me is to enable fast, robust, and accurate SLAM techniques on various hardware platforms, with the help from modern devices like GPU and ARM and state-of-the-art approaches like deep neural network.
